##
Course Description:
This course focuses on particle and continuum simulations to teach basic elements of scientific computing and visualization. Students gain experience in mathematical modeling, numerical solution methods, parallel computing, scientific visualization, and data analysis, with hands-on experience in parallel programming using MPI, OpenMP, and GPU programming (CUDA). The course also covers scientific big data and machine learning techniques.
